### Q: What are the new features?
A: See [README.md](README.md) - Key Features section

### Q: How do I save sessions?
A: Sessions auto-save. Click 📊 to view Dashboard.

### Q: How do I re-export data?
A: Click 📊 Dashboard > Find session > Click 📥 Export

### Q: What if it doesn't work?
A: See [QUICK_START.md](QUICK_START.md) - Common Issues section

### Q: How much data can I store?
A: Up to 50 sessions (~10MB total)

### Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes, all stored locally in browser. No cloud sync.
